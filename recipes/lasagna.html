Lasagna


This is the recipe of a sweet and savoury Lasagna, 100% non-vegan 100% fresh right from the kitchen

Recipe Ingredients:
  • Wheat flour
  • Chease
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Chilli Sauce
  • Vegetables
Steps:
  1. Knead the wheat dough with water and some olive Oil.
  2. Let that shit rest for a good ol while.
  3. flaten the dough with one big rolling pin.
  4. fill the Vegetables with a lota Cheese inside the flatened cut dough.
  5. Fry that hell out of it until everything looks crisp af.
  6. Serve hot.
